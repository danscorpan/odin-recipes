Kransekake (Almond Ring Cake)

Image source: https://assets.epicurious.com/photos/628d11141ca4c690c0969d76/1:1/w_2047,h_2047,c_limit/Kransekake%E2%80%94RECIPE.jpg

Description: A traditional Norwegian cake made from almond flour, often used for celebrations.

Ingredients:

  • 500 g (4 cups) almond flour
  • 250 g (2 cups) powdered sugar
  • 2 egg whites
  • 1 tsp almond extract
  • Icing sugar for decoration

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 175°C (350°F).
  2. In a bowl, mix almond flour, powdered sugar, egg whites, and almond extract to form a dough.
  3. Shape the dough into rings and place them on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper.
  4. Bake for about 15-20 minutes until lightly golden.
  5. Once cooled, stack the rings to create a tower and decorate with icing sugar.
