Koldtbord (Cold Table)

Image source: https://stikol.com/data/offerings/img/bo_WCRa19gbUw6K5p6JmbIYmdAcYCbsw/vuUKtIFQCG/image.jpg

Description: A traditional Norwegian smorgasbord featuring various cold dishes, such as cured meats, fish, and salads.

Ingredients:

  • Cured salmon (gravlaks)
  • Smoked mackerel
  • Boiled potatoes
  • Herring in various marinades
  • Cheese (Norwegian brown cheese is popular)
  • Rye bread
  • Fresh vegetables (such as cucumber and radishes)
  • Sour cream or herb dip

Instructions:

  1. Arrange the cured fish, meats, cheese, and vegetables on a large platter.
  2. Serve with rye bread and dips on the side.
  3. This dish is often enjoyed as part of festive occasions or gatherings.
